An online complaint redressal system is a platform designed to receive, track, and resolve customer complaints or grievances through the internet. The system is used by businesses, government organizations, and other service providers to provide customers with an efficient, transparent, and convenient way of registering their complaints.Some key features of an online complaint redressal system include:User-friendly interface: An easy-to-use and navigate platform that allows customers to easily register their complaints and track their resolution status.Multi-channel support: The ability to receive complaints through various channels such as email, web form, or mobile app.Automated complaint tracking: An automated system that tracks the status of complaints from receipt to resolution.
